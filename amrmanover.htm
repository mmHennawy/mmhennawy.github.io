Biography

Amr Hamdy

has Fourteen years experience in the field of Obstetrics and Gynecology as specialist after obtaining the MSc. degree from the faculty of medicine Ain Shams University, Cairo, Egypt in November 2000 and five years as a resident of Obstetrics and Gynecology from 1995 to 2000 in hospitals of the Ministry of Health of Egypt. He graduated in 1992 from Ain Shams Faculty of Medicine. He has wide practical experience in Obstetrics with more than 3,000 deliveries both normal and cesarean sections and also working in private clinic since 2007 and live in Cairo. He married and has three kids and has the hobbies of reading literature and novels and writing short stories
